| trafilatura network fetch | URLs fetched at ingest time from Premier Guitar; SSRF risk is accepted for single-user local tool |
| trafilatura extracted text | Treated as untrusted text; stored verbatim but not executed |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |

| T-06-09 | Spoofing | SSRF via article_urls.txt | accept | URLs are hardcoded in a committed file; this is a single-user local tool with no user-supplied URLs; ASVS V5 SSRF controls do not apply at this trust level |
| T-06-10 | Denial of Service | trafilatura hung fetch | accept | Single-user local tool; network hangs at ingest time are acceptable (not a latency-sensitive path); OS TCP timeout will eventually fire |
